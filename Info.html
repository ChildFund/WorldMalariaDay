How ChildFund Fights Malaria

At ChildFund, we are adding to the global effort to combat malaria by
  • Educating children and families about malaria prevention
  • Providing communities with insecticide-treated mosquito nets
  • Focusing on the most vulnerable: pregnant women, households headed by children and grandparents, and those with disabilities

Mosquito nets are an inexpensive yet effective way of preventing malaria. Nets typically last 3-5 years and provide protection for the most vulnerable.
